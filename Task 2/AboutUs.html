About Us

The story behind Acome Co.

Our Story

Acme Co. began in 2015 as a small workshop with one simple idea: make things people will actually keep.Our founder grew tired of disposable products and wanted to bring back the quailty of an earlier era - when things were built to last and made by hands that cared.

Over a decade later,we're grown into a team of designers,makers ,and cutomers advocates,but the original idea hasn't changed.We still make every product as if we were going to use it ourselves.

Our Mission

To craft everyday goods that earn their place in your life — through quality materials, honest design, and the kind of finish you can feel in your hands.

Our Values

  1. Craft - We design slowly and build carefully.
  2. Honesty - We tell you what something is made of.
  3. Longevity - Products should outlive trends.
  4. People - Real customers, real makers, real conversations.
