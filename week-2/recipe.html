Perfect Homemade Pizza

The best recipe for a crispy and delicious crust

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (160g) All-purpose flour [00:00:20]
  • 1 tsp Yeast [00:00:30]
  • 1 tsp Sugar [00:00:36]
  • 1/4 tsp Salt [00:00:43]
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil [00:00:58]
  • 3 fl oz (90ml) Warm water [00:01:08]

Preparation Steps

  1. Combine flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl and stir [00:00:50].
  2. Add olive oil and warm water, then mix until a dough forms [00:01:15].
  3. Knead the dough for 2-3 minutes until soft but not sticky [00:01:25].
  4. Grease a bowl with oil and let the dough rise in a warm place for 40-60 minutes [00:01:45].
  5. Prepare the fresh tomato sauce by blanching 14 oz of tomatoes [00:02:10].
